Our Service Commitment

The promises we make to you

We agree the level of service you require in advance, and we work hard to deliver it at every stage of your matter.

What you can expect from us

Whatever your matter, we commit to the following standards for every client.

Clear advice

We will give you clear legal advice that you can understand, and make sure you know what we are doing and your prospects of success.

Agreed expectations

We will agree with you in advance the expectations of service you require, and represent your interests accordingly.

Transparent costs

We will be clear about the likely costs involved, including how these can be funded, and only charge in accordance with the advice given.

Regular updates

We will keep you regularly informed of progress and any developments on your matter.

Confidentiality

We will protect your confidentiality at all times, handling your matter with discretion and care.

A prompt response

We will respond promptly to your correspondence, calls and enquiries, and deal with any complaints quickly.

If something goes wrong

Our complaints procedure

We are committed to providing a high-quality legal service to all of our clients. If at any point you are unhappy with the service you have received, we want to hear from you so that we can put things right.

We will deal with any complaint promptly, fairly and free of charge. In the first instance, please raise your concern with the person handling your matter, or with the partner responsible. If you would like a copy of our full complaints procedure, please contact either office and we will be happy to provide it.

Contact us about a complaint
Contact the person dealing with your matter, or the responsible partner, by phone, email or letter. Tell us what has gone wrong and how you would like it resolved.
We will acknowledge your complaint promptly and aim to investigate and respond within the timescales set out in our full complaints procedure.
