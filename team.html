Lucy Ardern

Head of Family & COFA
Family LawMember of ResolutionCOFA

Lucy was admitted as a solicitor in 1993 and joined Copleys in 1999, becoming a partner in 2001. She has over 30 years' experience in all areas of matrimonial law and divorce, particularly in complex financial disputes and settlements. Her wealth of experience includes:

  • High value financial settlements and pensions
  • Co-habitation agreements and disputes
  • Disputes involving children, including residence and contact
  • Court of Appeal proceedings

Lucy adopts an understanding and conciliatory approach but aims to achieve the best outcome for her clients, mindful of costs at all times. She is a member of Resolution and is Copleys' Compliance Officer for Financial Administration (COFA).

Paula Hamilton

Partner & COLP
Family LawResolution Collaborative LawyerCOLP

Paula is a solicitor with over 30 years' experience, based at the firm's St Ives office. She joined Copleys in 2009 and was made Partner in 2013. She specialises in all areas of family law including divorce, complex financial and property matters and cases relating to children, and is a trained Resolution Collaborative Lawyer.

Not only is Paula a highly experienced lawyer; she is also approachable, professional but sensitive, and able to provide advice and support in a pragmatic and compassionate manner. Her particular focus includes:

  • Divorce
  • Divorce finances — negotiating and drafting settlements in Consent Orders, from straightforward agreements to complex cases involving property portfolios, business assets, pensions and pension sharing
  • Children — disputes over where children live, parental responsibility, relocation, change of name and taking children abroad
