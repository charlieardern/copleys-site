Who We Are

Looking after our clients' best interests since 1890

Copleys is a firm of solicitors based in Huntingdon and St Ives, providing legal services to clients throughout East Anglia and Cambridgeshire.

Our Story

More than 130 years of trusted advice

Copleys was established in 1890 and has developed its standing by looking after our clients' best interests. Over the generations, that simple principle has earned us a reputation built on quality, hard work and supporting clients through every step of their journey.

Our practice is organised into two divisions. Our commercial team offers business consultation and legal advisory services across a wide variety of industry sectors in the Cambridgeshire region. Our Private Client division delivers specialist legal assistance to individuals — functioning as personal advisers across generations of families in Cambridgeshire.

From two offices in Huntingdon and St Ives, we combine the depth of a long-established firm with a genuinely personal, approachable service.
