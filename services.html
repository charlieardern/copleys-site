Residential Property

Moving Home

Moving home can be a stressful process, often with unexpected twists and turns. Our experienced property team is here to make sure your transaction proceeds as smoothly as possible, putting your interests first, with professional service and personal attention, and keeping you informed throughout.

We can help with all aspects of residential conveyancing, including:

  • Buying or selling residential property
  • Buying or selling investment property
  • Tenancy agreements
  • Re-mortgages
  • Transfers of equity
  • Deeds of gift
  • Planning issues
  • Lease extensions

This service suits anyone planning to move house and needing advice on conveyancing, or on any other aspect of property ownership.

Family Law

Family law covers a wide range of matters, including divorce, children law and financial separation issues. When disagreements arise, professional guidance helps ensure fair outcomes. We are approachable and friendly, and we will be in your corner from the start of proceedings to the end, always making sure your best interests are at the forefront.

Our partners Lucy Ardern and Paula Hamilton each have over 30 years' experience working exclusively in family law. They regularly advise on:

  • Divorce
  • Financial settlements
  • Children
  • Civil partnerships
  • Unmarried couples and cohabitation
  • Grandparent advice
  • Domestic abuse
  • Prenuptial agreements

Both are members of Resolution, which promotes the resolution of disputes in a constructive and non-confrontational way, with a view to keeping your costs to a minimum. We offer initial fixed-fee consultations to help you understand your options and likely costs.

Wills, Probate & Trusts

Our Wills & Probate department provides comprehensive estate planning and administration services, giving you peace of mind that your wishes will be carried out. Our work includes:

  • Will drafting
  • Administration of estates, including intestacies
  • The creation of trusts
  • Powers of attorney and Office of the Public Guardian matters
  • Inheritance tax and capital gains tax planning

How we charge

We explain our pricing to each client individually, and estimates will be provided whenever possible as to the likely total cost. Fixed fees are available for straightforward wills and lasting powers of attorney, with charges otherwise reflecting the complexity and time involved.

How it works

We prioritise understanding your needs through an initial meeting. A dedicated fee earner manages the day-to-day work on your file, while a partner retains overall responsibility for each matter. Our Wills, Probate & Trusts department is led by Alan Smith.

Commercial Property

Our Commercial Property team handles the whole range of commercial property issues. We act on the acquisition and disposal of all types of commercial property, including offices, shops, factories, licensed premises and farmland, both freehold and leasehold, as well as land for development and redevelopment.

Where premises are critical to your operation, we make sure your interests are properly protected, handling matters smoothly and efficiently and supporting you to achieve your objectives within your timescales, however urgent.

Competitive, transparent pricing

We discuss fees with you upfront, tailoring charges to the type of transaction. We offer a competitive service and give clear details of hourly rates and all anticipated expenses, including search fees and stamp duties.

We act for clients of every size, from small self-managed businesses and medium-sized local and regional companies to publicly listed corporations operating nationally, including London-based firms. The team is led by Samantha Jewell, Head of Commercial Property and Commercial Services, with Partner Mark Woolsey.

Business

Services for Business

If you are starting up or running your own business, it is important that you understand the legislation and practicalities that directly affect you. We deliver professional, practical and commercially focused advice for successful business development, helping you build solid legal foundations.

We organise our business services into five areas:

  • Setup: company formation, share structures, partnership agreements, IT and human resources.
  • Property: sales, purchases, leasing, development land, investment property and landlord & tenant issues.
  • People: employment law, contracts, health & safety, manuals, training, policies and terms and conditions.
  • Finance & Tax: loans, mortgages, debt collection, pensions, wills, financial planning, key-man insurance and dispute resolution.
  • Regulation & Licensing: keeping you compliant as you grow.

Following an initial consultation, we analyse your circumstances and deliver tailored recommendations, providing both problem-solving guidance and strategic business planning support, whatever your size or industry.
